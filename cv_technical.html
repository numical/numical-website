Profile

I am a rounded, commercially orientated technical team leader with over 20 years experience in the business of software development over a wide range of different industries.

I have written a lot of code, architected systems, led technical development teams onshore and offshore, and project managed.

I view leadership as the opportunity to build high-functioning teams and to grow engineers.

My core technical skills are enterprise open-source technologies in JavaScript and Java. I have applied these to all levels of the technical stack from User Interface to Database and under project management methodologies ranging from waterfall, through DSDM to agile approaches such as Scrum, Kanban and XP.

Technically, I aspire to be that mythical beast, a genuine full-stack developer.

I regard as core competences the soft skills of listening hard, communicating well, and motivating others. I work particularly well at the interface between business and technology. My main driver is always delivery.
