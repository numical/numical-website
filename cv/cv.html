Profile

I am a rounded, commercially orientated technical architect and team lead with over 19 years experience of the business of software development in a wide range of different industries. I have architected systems, led technical development teams onshore and offshore, and project managed. My core technical skills are enterprise open-source technologies in Java and JavaScript. I have applied these under project management methodologies ranging from traditional waterfall, through DSDM to modern agile approaches such as Scrum and XP.

In addition to my technical skills, I regard as core competences the soft skills of listening hard, communicating well, and motivating others. I work particularly well at the interface between business and technology. My principle concern is delivery.

After a two-year break being a Dad, I am looking to return to the industry as a full-stack Javascript engineer.
