Profile

I am a rounded, commercially orientated technical architect and team lead with over 20 years experience of the business of software development in a wide range of different industries. I have architected systems, led technical development teams onshore and offshore, and project managed.

My core technical skills are enterprise open-source technologies in JavaScript and Java. I have applied these to all levels of the technical stack from User Interface to Database and under project management methodologies ranging from waterfall, through DSDM to agile approaches such as Scrum and XP.

In addition to my technical skills, I regard as core competences the soft skills of listening hard, communicating well, and motivating others. I work particularly well at the interface between business and technology. My principle concern is delivery.

After a two-year break being a Dad, I am looking to return to the industry as a full-stack Javascript engineer, a role I consider particularly exciting at the moment.

Core Technologies

Bright Grey sells financial protection products through web, telephone, fax and mail channels. This involves complex workflows with multiple interactions between telephony, IT and manual systems. I was employed to analyse the operations of the technical support team, and report on ways to improve these. I was also asked to investigate flaws and suggest fixes to the business processes.
